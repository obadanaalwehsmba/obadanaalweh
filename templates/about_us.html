About Us

Welcome to our Text-to-Speech service! Our mission is to provide a free and unlimited platform for converting text to speech. Our goal is to make communication easier and more accessible for everyone.

We believe in offering a service that can be used by anyone, at any time, without restrictions. Whether you're looking to listen to your favorite articles or convert your written content into speech, we're here to help.

Our team is dedicated to ensuring the service remains free and easy to use. If you'd like to support us, consider donating to help keep the service running smoothly.
